ROUND 1 : BRAIN BATTLE

This round will cover a range of topics, like mathematics and logical reasoning. Participants will have to answer multiple-choice questions within a specified time frame. The primary objective of this round is to challenge the participants' cognitive abilities and identify the most adept teams that will advance to the subsequent rounds of the competition.


ROUND 2 : QUIZTOPHER

The second round of is an interactive audio-visual quiz. The quiz will have a mix of easy and challenging questions that will require the participants to think creatively and work with their teammates. This round is designed to be engaging and entertaining while still challenging the participants' abilities. The top-performing teams from this round will advance to the final round of the competition.


ROUND 3 : SHERLOCKED

The third and final round is an exciting online escape room challenge that will test the participants' problem-solving skills, creativity, and teamwork to their optimum levels. It will consist of a series of puzzles and riddles that the participants must solve within a stipulated time frame. The teams must work together and leverage their collective skills and knowledge to escape the virtual room successfully. This round is designed to be the most thrilling and challenging of the three and will determine the ultimate winners of the competition.
